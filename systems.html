Scope

Everything that's included.

Every Systems engagement covers the full operational infrastructure behind your business — from the moment a visitor converts to the moment you get paid. No manual follow-up, no dropped leads, no chasing invoices.

  • CRM setup and client pipeline configuration
  • Form submission routing — notifications, tagging, and lead organization
  • Stripe payment logic, invoicing, and subscription management
  • Supabase database setup and data architecture
  • Email automations and follow-up sequences
  • Calendar integrations and booking confirmations
  • Custom dashboards so you can see your business data in real time
  • One round of revisions after delivery

Boundaries

What this service doesn't cover.

Systems does not touch anything the visitor sees on your website. If you need a website built or redesigned, that is a Builds engagement. If you need both, Full Stack is the right offer.

  • Website design or front-end development
  • Page layout, typography, or visual design
  • Contact form UI or booking page design
  • Payment page design or checkout experience
  • Netlify deployment or domain configuration
  • Mobile optimization or performance tuning

The Process

How a Systems engagement works.

01

Audit

We start by mapping your current tools, workflows, and gaps. We need to understand what you're already using before we build anything new.

02

Architecture

We design the full systems map — what connects to what, what triggers what, and what the data flow looks like end to end.

03

Build & Connect

We configure your CRM, automations, database, and integrations. Every connection is tested before we move to the next.

04

Handoff & Training

We walk you through everything that was built, how to use it, and how to maintain it. You leave with full ownership and documentation.
