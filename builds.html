Scope

Everything that's included.

Every Builds engagement covers the full front-end experience — from the first page the visitor lands on to the moment they convert. No hidden scope, no surprise add-ons.

  • Brand-aligned custom design — built to reflect your business identity, not a template
  • Full page structure and copy layout
  • Contact forms and booking UI — the form itself, not the backend logic
  • Payment pages and checkout UI — the page, not the Stripe configuration
  • Netlify deployment and custom domain setup
  • Mobile-first build, optimized for performance
  • One round of revisions after delivery
  • Ongoing maintenance available as an add-on — content updates, design changes, page additions

Boundaries

What this service doesn't cover.

Knowing what's out of scope protects both of us. If you need any of the following, Purple Feather Systems or Full Stack is the right engagement.

  • CRM setup or client pipeline configuration
  • What happens after a form is submitted — routing, tagging, notifications
  • Stripe payment logic or invoice automation
  • Supabase database setup or data architecture
  • Email automations or follow-up sequences
  • Custom dashboards or reporting tools

The Process

How a Builds engagement works.

01

Discovery

We start with a conversation about your brand, your goals, and what you need the site to do. No templates, no assumptions.

02

Design

We establish the visual direction — layout, typography, color, and structure — before a single line of code is written.

03

Build

We build your site in vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS, deployed on Netlify. You see progress and approve before we finalize.

04

Launch

We deploy to your domain, hand off all assets, and walk you through everything you own. You leave with full control of your site.
